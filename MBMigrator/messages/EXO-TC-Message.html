Reminders:
  • Webmail: If you are currently authorized to use Webmail (OWA) and are not on the VPN, you will need to access outlook.office.comand sign in using your email address and AD password. You will also need your RSA token code for multi-factor authentication.
  • Shared Mailboxes: If you are unable to access a shared mailbox, contact the EXO migration team.
  • Delegation permissions: If they are not set up accurately, review the Outlook Delegate Access Guide or Contact the EXO Migration Team.
  • For selected Distribution Groups Owners only: If you previously received a notification, remember to use your group email address when sending messages to the group.

ACTION REQUIRED: Mobile
After migration, you must log on to MaaS360 to invoke the quarantine process. This process is required to reauthenticate your user account with your migrated mailbox. You will receive an email stating "Your device is temporarily blocked from accessing content via Exchange ActiveSync because the device has been quarantined." You do not need to take any action. Access is granted automatically within a few minutes and email will begin populating. Based on your cell signal/network connection, and amount of email and calendar events, resyncing can take up to two hours.
