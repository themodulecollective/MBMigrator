We are excited to announce the Workspace Transformation and our journey to Microsoft 365. An essential step in this journey is to migrate your on-premises mailbox to Exchange Online. That means the contents of your mailboxes, conference rooms, calendars, and public folders will move to the Cloud.
Over the next few weeks, you will receive a series of messages related to your Outlook mailbox migration. These messages contain 'Need to Know' information to guide you through a good migration experience. You shouldn't notice anything different in Outlook, and there should be minimal disruption to your work.

What's next?
  • No action on your part is necessary at this time — the process is fully automated.
  • Our request — starting now, do not reorganize, clean-up, or make any significant changes to your mailbox folder structure.
  • You will receive an email next week with your Outlook migration date.
  • You will receive an email on the day of migration.
  • After your email migration is complete, the entire mailbox is moved; there will be no changes to your folder structure and content.
We are working diligently to make this transition as smooth as possible, but we need your help!

What can you do to help?
  • Be aware. Watch for the email next week for your mailbox migration date.
  • Stay informed: Visit the Workspace Transformation site
