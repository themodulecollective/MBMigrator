Your Mailbox Migration begins at 9:00 PM EST Tonight!

What you need to know:
  • For optimal results, take the night off email.
  • Your migration happens seamlessly in the background.
  • Please note below impact to MaaS360 users!
  • If necessary, you can continue working while your email is migrating.
  • If you are in Outlook, you may receive an Outlook prompt:

After migration is completed:
  • Sign on: Open the Outlook application on your computer.
    If you are prompted to enter your credentials, clear any prepopulated information and sign in with firstname.lastname@domain.com and associated password.
    Note: for some exceptions, users need to sign in with empID@domain.com.
  • You will receive an email noting that your migration has been completed.

ACTION REQUIRED: Mobile
After migration, you must log on to MaaS360 to invoke the quarantine process. This process is required to reauthenticate your user account with your migrated mailbox. You will receive an email stating "Your device is temporarily blocked from accessing content via Exchange ActiveSync because the device has been quarantined." You do not need to take any action. Access is granted automatically within a few minutes and email will begin populating. Based on your cell signal/network connection, and amount of email and calendar events, resyncing can take up to two hours.

Note: You may need to reconfigure your Personal Preferences If you have made any changes such as your email signature, days to sync, etc... This is optional.
Review the MaaS360 Guide for more information regarding Personal Preferences.

What else?
  • Calendars and Meetings: Your calendar, meetings, and dial-in conference information will also migrate and will be available for your use.
  • Shared Mailboxes: Continue to access shared mailboxes in the same manner as you currently do. If you are unable to access a shared mailbox, review the Exchange Online and Outlook FAQs for tips on resolving the issue or contact the EXO migration team.
  • Delegation: If your Delegation permissions are not set up accurately, review the Outlook Delegate Access Guide or contact EXO Migration Team.
  • Public Folders: Continue to access public folders in the same manner as you currently do. Public Folders reside on-premises and will migrate after all mailboxes are moved. If you are unable to access a Public Folder, review the Exchange Online and Outlook FAQs for tips on resolving the issue or contact the EXO migration team.
  • Webmail: If you are currently authorized to use Webmail (OWA) and are not on the VPN, you will need to access outlook.office.com and sign in using your email address and AD password. You will also need your RSA token code for multi-factor authentication.
  • *New* Groups feature in Outlook: your folder structure contains a section called Groups. If you are using Teams, a group email address is displayed containing the members for each Teams workspace of which you are a member, also displayed are addresses. Review the Groups Feature in Outlook Guide for more information.
  • Distribution Groups: If you are a Distribution Group Owner, review the Managing Distribution Group's Guide to learn how to manage members. For new DG's, please continue to use the TSC Self Service Portal request form.
  • Reminder: Selected Distribution Groups Owners only: If you previously received a notification, remember to use your group email address when sending messages.
